# AI chatbot functionality

1. **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**: AI chatbots utilize NLP to understand and interpret human language. This allows them to comprehend user queries, extract relevant information, and generate appropriate responses.
2. **Machine Learning (ML)**: AI chatbots employ ML algorithms to learn from interactions and improve their responses over time. They can adapt to user preferences and provide personalized experiences.
3. **Rule-Based Systems**: Some chatbots rely on predefined rules and scripts to handle specific types of queries. These systems are useful for straightforward tasks but may struggle with complex or open-ended questions.
4. **Information Database**: Chatbots often integrate with databases to access and provide information. This can include product catalogs, FAQs, or user profiles. By leveraging structured data, chatbots can offer more accurate and relevant responses.
5. **Deep Learning**: Advanced chatbots may utilize deep learning techniques, such as neural networks, to enhance their understanding and generation of human language. This allows them to handle more complex conversations and provide more natural responses.
