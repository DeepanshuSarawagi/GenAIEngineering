# AI Agents

## Introduction

AI Agents are software agents that can be used to perform tasks. They can be used to automate tasks, provide information, and interact with users. 

AI agents can be classified into different types based on their capabilities and functionalities.

They can enable decision-making, problem-solving, and learning. They can be used in various applications such as customer service, virtual assistants, and autonomous systems.

### Multi-Agent Systems

Multi-agent systems are systems that involve multiple agents working together to achieve a common goal.
Multi-agent systems can be used to solve complex problems, such as traffic management, resource allocation, and collaborative tasks. They can also be used to simulate social interactions and behaviors.
